Our Story

Aranya's Kitchen has been setup in 2020 with the aim to serve the best flavours available in the field of baking.Yes you are correct!Aranya's kitchen is a home bakery owned by chef Arti.With a wide range of cakes, her specialties include Chocolate cakes, Cheesecakes, fondants, cupcakes,Donuts,cookies etc.


A management post-graduate by education,Arti always had a passion for cooking.Exploring from the home kitchen to the launch of Home bakery,it took long time to convert her passion into profession.Though not professionally trained chef,she explored her passion in the field of cooking and finally ventured baking.She churned up her knowledge,skills and passion to create mouth watering Bakery products and finally established Aranya's kitchen in the name of her Son and daughter(Aarav and Ananya).
